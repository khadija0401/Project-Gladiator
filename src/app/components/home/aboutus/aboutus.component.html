About National Scholarship Portal

The National Scholarship Portal is new way for the government providing scholarship schemes by the state government and central government. NSP portal provides 118 scholarships, which helps poor and backward students such as SC, ST, and OBC who can't pursue their education due to financial situations or covid. “Mission Mode Project” is under the National e-Governance Plan as the academic session 2022-23. NSP disburses scholarships amount around INR 2,800 Crores to support the students.

University Grants Commission, UGC recently released NSP Scholarship 2022-23 or scholarships.gov.in Portal notification. Every year, NSP introduces nearly 80 scholarship schemes for students coming from different backgrounds and also from different education levels.

Through National Scholarship Portal, candidates can get education who are from backward classes or can't pursue their education due to financial crisis. Candidate must fill the application form before the NSP Scholarship 2022-23 last date i.e. is August 31, 2022.
