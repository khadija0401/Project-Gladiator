Portal Description
  • The National Scholarship portal is new way for the government providing scholarship schemes by the central government. NSP portal provides 10 scholarships, which helps backward students such as SC, ST, and OBC who can't pursue their education due to financial situations or covid.

  • National Scholarships Portal is taken as Mission Mode Project under National e-Governance Plan.
Guidelines
  • Applicant should register on National Scholarships Portal
  • Applicant should login into National Scholarships Portal
  • Applicant should click apply button for suitable scholarship scheme
  • Applicant should fill all the details in scholarship form
  • Before applying application to any scheme for academic year 2022-23, all your applications from previous year i.e. academic year 2021-22 should be disposed. Disposed means the status of the application must be Approved, Rejected or Granted. Please note, you cannot apply the application for academic year 2022-23 if your application is in under scrutiny.
